Spaghetti Carbonara

Ingredients:

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 100g pancetta or guanciale
  • 2 large eggs
  • 50g Pecorino Romano cheese
  • Salt and black pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Boil spaghetti according to package instructions.
  2. Fry pancetta or guanciale in a pan until crispy.
  3. In a bowl, beat eggs and mix in grated Pecorino Romano cheese.
  4. Drain cooked spaghetti and immediately mix with egg and cheese mixture.
  5. Add crispy pancetta/guanciale and season with salt and pepper. Toss to combine.
  6. Serve hot and garnish with extra cheese and black pepper.

Chicken Alfredo

Ingredients:

  • 8 oz fettuccine pasta
  • 2 chicken breasts, cooked and sliced
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Cook pasta according to package instructions.
  2. In a saucepan, heat heavy cream and Parmesan cheese, stirring until smooth.
  3. Season with salt and pepper, then add sliced chicken and cooked pasta.
  4. Toss to combine, and serve hot.

Veggie Stir-Fry

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups mixed vegetables
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • Rice or noodles for serving

Instructions:

  1. Heat sesame oil in a pan and sauté minced garlic.
  2. Add mixed vegetables and stir-fry until tender-crisp.
  3. Stir in soy sauce and cook for a few more minutes.
  4. Serve over rice or noodles.

Tiramisu

Ingredients:

  • 6 egg yolks
  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 cup mascarpone cheese
  • 1 1/2 cups strong brewed coffee
  • Ladyfingers and cocoa powder

Instructions:

  1. Whisk egg yolks and sugar until creamy.
  2. Fold in mascarpone cheese to create a smooth mixture.
  3. Dip ladyfingers in coffee and layer them with the mascarpone mixture.
  4. Repeat layers and chill for a few hours.
  5. Dust with cocoa powder before serving.

Butter Chicken

Ingredients:

  • 500g boneless chicken
  • 1 cup plain yogurt
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 teaspoons garam masala
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Marinate chicken in yogurt and spices for at least 2 hours.
  2. In a pan, melt butter and sauté onions until golden brown.
  3. Add marinated chicken and cook until done.
  4. Serve with naan or rice.

Vegetable Biryani

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • Assorted vegetables
  • 2 teaspoons biryani masala
  • 1/4 cup yogurt
  • Fresh mint leaves and saffron strands

Instructions:

  1. Cook rice and keep aside.
  2. Sauté vegetables and biryani masala in a pan.
  3. Mix with cooked rice, yogurt, and garnishes.
  4. Steam the biryani for a few minutes before serving.
